Water Footprint

Brushing and Washbasin Use for 10 Min : 120 Litres
Shower (overhead) for 15 Mins : 180 Litres
Flush Lines : 6 times * 16 Litres : 96 Litres
Dish Washing for 20 Mins : 240 Litres (12 Litres/Minute)
Washing Clothes : 4 Buckets * 16 Litres : 64 Litres
Mopping : 2 Buckets : 32 Litres
Cooking and Drinking Water : 15 litres

Grand Total : 747 Litres


Product Interventions

3 Litres/minute aerators save 80% water in hand wash areas.
Hand Shower can help save 30% water
Shower restrictors can help reduce wastage


Case Study : MLZS

Assesment at Mount Litera Zee School showed water loss in restrooms with flow rates of 8 to 12 Litres per minute Engaged high school students to conduct water audits
Students implemented aerators in all hand wash taps
savings of average 40 Litres of water from each tap (Average 5 Minute Usage)
Daily savings of 3600 Litres of Water/day
Savings of 108000 Litres/month
Savings also observed in electricity for water pumping, filtration unit, etc.
